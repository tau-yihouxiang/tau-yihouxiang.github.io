Biography [CV]

I am currently a PhD Candinate at Computer Science & Engineering Department, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), under the supervision of Prof. Jiaya Jia. Before that, I obtained the Master degree in Computer Science, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) in 2019, supervised by Prof. Honggang Qi. I received the Bachelor degree in Automation Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2016.

Recently, I founded RealityEditor which combines Large Model with Interaction for creative work.

My current research interests lie primarily in Computer Vision and Graphics, particularly focusing on:

Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF), 2D image / 3D Scene Synthesis, 3D Rendering and Reconstruction.
